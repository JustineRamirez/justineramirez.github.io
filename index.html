Hold your wallets, ladies! Before you begin picking which lipstick you want, you must start with something essential: the lipstick finish. You want to make sure the lipstick you pick has the perfect texture and result that suits you. There are many lipstick finishes, but here are a few of the basic ones:

  1. Matte

    2. The infamous matte finish, raved by many. If you are one that prefers less gloss and more longevity, this lipstick finish is for you. Matte finishes provide a flat pigment on the lips, leaving little hydration but insane color payoff.

  2. Creamy

    3. These finshes are the perfect combination of matte and glossy (we'll delve into that one later on). Unlike matte finishes, these will be able to give you some of the moisture of a lip balm and the long-lasting pigment of a matte.
